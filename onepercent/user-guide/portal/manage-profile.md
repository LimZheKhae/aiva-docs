# Manage your profile

Update your personal information, upload a profile photo, and view your payment history.

## View your profile

1. Tap the **Profile** tab in the bottom navigation.

Your profile page shows:

- **Profile card** — your avatar, name, phone number, branch, and membership duration.
- **Stats** — total points, total payments, and completed sessions (tappable to see details).
- **Menu** — links to Edit Profile, My Points, and Payment History.

<!-- ![Profile page overview](./assets/manage-profile-overview.png) -->

## Edit your profile

1. Tap **Edit Profile** on the profile page.
1. Update any of these fields:

| Field | Editable? |
| --- | --- |
| **Profile photo** | Yes — tap to upload a new image. |
| **Full Name** | Yes |
| **Email** | No — contact staff to change. |
| **Phone** | No — contact staff to change. |
| **Date of Birth** | Yes |
| **Gender** | Yes |
| **Emergency Contact 1** | Yes (name, phone, relationship). Required. |
| **Emergency Contact 2** | Yes. Optional. |

3. Tap **Save** at the bottom.

<!-- ![Edit profile form](./assets/manage-profile-edit.png) -->

### Upload a profile photo

1. Tap your current avatar or the photo placeholder.
1. Select an image from your device.
1. A preview appears.
1. Tap **Save** — the photo uploads first, then the profile saves.

## View payment history

1. Tap **Payment History** on the profile page (or tap the **Payments** stat card).

<!-- ![Payment history with filters](./assets/manage-profile-payments.png) -->

### Features

- **Total Paid** — your lifetime payment total.
- **Outstanding Balance** — shown if you have any remaining balance.
- **Search** — find payments by package name or invoice number. Matching text is highlighted.
- **Year and month filters** — scroll through months to find specific payments.
- **Invoice download** — tap the download icon on any payment with an invoice. The PDF downloads automatically.
- **Load More** — payments load 20 at a time.

## Change your password

1. Tap the **Profile** tab.
1. Navigate to **Settings** or use the portal login page.
1. Tap **Forgot Password** on the login page.
1. Enter your email and tap **Send reset link**.
1. Check your email and tap the link.
1. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters) and confirm.

:::note
There's no "Change Password" button inside the portal once you're logged in. Use the forgot password flow to reset your password.
:::

## Sign out

1. Scroll to the bottom of the profile page.
1. Tap **Sign Out**.
1. You're redirected to the login page.

## Things to keep in mind

- Email and phone number can only be changed by gym staff from the admin app.
- Emergency Contact 1 is required — you can't save your profile without it.
- Your profile photo is stored in Supabase Storage. It may take a moment to appear after uploading.
- Payment history shows all payments across all your packages — past and present.
